# Nutrition Estimation Guidelines

## Portion Size Reference

- **Palm of hand** ≈ 3-4 oz protein (chicken, fish, meat)
- **Fist** ≈ 1 cup (rice, pasta, vegetables)
- **Cupped hand** ≈ 1/2 cup (nuts, grains)
- **Thumb** ≈ 1 tablespoon (butter, oil, peanut butter)
- **Standard plate** ≈ 10-11 inches diameter

## Common Macro Estimates

### Proteins (per 4 oz / 113g cooked)
- Chicken breast: 185 cal, 35g protein, 0g carbs, 4g fat
- Salmon: 235 cal, 25g protein, 0g carbs, 14g fat
- Ground beef (85/15): 240 cal, 28g protein, 0g carbs, 13g fat
- Eggs (1 large): 70 cal, 6g protein, 0.5g carbs, 5g fat
- Tofu (firm, 4 oz): 90 cal, 10g protein, 2g carbs, 5g fat

### Carbs (per 1 cup cooked)
- White rice: 205 cal, 4g protein, 45g carbs, 0.5g fat
- Brown rice: 215 cal, 5g protein, 45g carbs, 1.5g fat
- Pasta: 220 cal, 8g protein, 43g carbs, 1g fat
- Oatmeal: 155 cal, 5g protein, 27g carbs, 3g fat
- Sweet potato (1 medium): 115 cal, 2g protein, 27g carbs, 0g fat

### Fats
- Olive oil (1 tbsp): 120 cal, 0g protein, 0g carbs, 14g fat
- Avocado (1/2): 160 cal, 2g protein, 9g carbs, 15g fat
- Peanut butter (2 tbsp): 190 cal, 7g protein, 7g carbs, 16g fat

### Common Meals (approximate)
- Fast food burger (single): 400-550 cal
- Slice of pizza (regular): 250-350 cal
- Burrito (standard): 500-800 cal
- Salad with dressing: 300-600 cal
- Sandwich (deli): 350-550 cal

## Exercise Calorie Burn Estimates (per 30 min, ~170 lb person)

- Running (6 mph): 320 cal
- Cycling (moderate): 260 cal
- Swimming (laps): 250 cal
- Weight training: 130 cal
- Walking (3.5 mph): 150 cal
- Yoga: 120 cal
- HIIT: 300 cal
- Rowing: 260 cal
- Jump rope: 340 cal
- Elliptical: 200 cal

_Scale estimates proportionally based on user's reported weight if known._
