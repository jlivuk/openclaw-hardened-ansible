# Home Maintenance Reference Data

## Recommended Maintenance Intervals

### HVAC System
- **Air filter replacement**: Every 30-90 days (30 if pets, 60 standard, 90 high-quality filters)
- **Professional HVAC tune-up**: Twice per year (spring for AC, fall for heating)
- **Duct cleaning**: Every 3-5 years
- **Thermostat battery**: Annually
- **Expected lifespan**: 15-25 years (central air), 10-15 years (heat pump)

### Water Heater
- **Flush/drain tank**: Annually (prevents sediment buildup)
- **Anode rod inspection**: Every 2-3 years (replace if corroded)
- **Temperature check**: Annually (should be 120°F / 49°C)
- **Expected lifespan**: 8-12 years (tank), 15-20 years (tankless)

### Plumbing
- **Water softener salt**: Monthly check
- **Sump pump test**: Quarterly (pour water to verify operation)
- **Main water shut-off valve**: Test annually (turn off and on to prevent seizing)
- **Hose bib/outdoor faucet**: Winterize before first freeze
- **Washing machine hoses**: Replace every 3-5 years

### Electrical
- **Smoke detector batteries**: Every 6 months (or when chirping)
- **Smoke detector replacement**: Every 10 years
- **CO detector replacement**: Every 5-7 years
- **GFCI outlet test**: Monthly (press test/reset buttons)
- **Electrical panel inspection**: Every 5 years (by licensed electrician)

### Exterior
- **Gutter cleaning**: Twice per year (spring and fall)
- **Roof inspection**: Annually (visual) + after major storms
- **Exterior paint/stain**: Every 5-10 years
- **Driveway sealing**: Every 2-3 years (asphalt)
- **Deck/fence staining**: Every 2-3 years
- **Power washing**: Annually (siding, walkways)
- **Window caulking check**: Annually

### Appliances
- **Refrigerator coils cleaning**: Every 6-12 months
- **Dishwasher filter cleaning**: Monthly
- **Dryer vent cleaning**: Annually (fire prevention)
- **Oven self-clean or deep clean**: Quarterly
- **Garbage disposal cleaning**: Monthly (ice + citrus method)
- **Range hood filter**: Monthly cleaning, replace every 1-2 years
- **Washing machine**: Monthly cleaning cycle (empty with hot water + cleaner)

### Seasonal
- **Winterization**: Before first freeze — disconnect hoses, insulate pipes, check weather stripping
- **Spring check-up**: After last freeze — inspect roof, clean gutters, service AC, check exterior for winter damage
- **Hurricane prep** (if applicable): Before hurricane season — check shutters, clear drains, secure loose items, backup generator fuel
- **Fire prevention**: Fall — clean chimney, check smoke detectors, clear leaves from roof/gutters

## Typical Service Costs (US averages, 2024-2025)

### HVAC
- Filter replacement: $5-40 (DIY) per filter
- Professional tune-up: $75-200 per visit
- Duct cleaning: $300-700
- Full system replacement: $5,000-15,000

### Plumbing
- Water heater flush: $80-200 (professional), $0 DIY
- Drain cleaning: $100-350
- Water heater replacement: $800-2,500 (tank), $1,500-4,500 (tankless)
- Sump pump replacement: $500-1,200

### Electrical
- Smoke/CO detectors: $15-60 each
- GFCI outlet: $15-25 (DIY) or $100-200 (electrician)
- Panel inspection: $100-250

### Exterior
- Gutter cleaning: $100-250 (professional), $0 DIY
- Roof inspection: $150-400
- Power washing: $200-500
- Deck staining: $500-1,500 (professional), $100-300 DIY

### Appliances
- Dryer vent cleaning: $100-200
- Refrigerator coil cleaning: $0 DIY, $100-150 professional
- Dishwasher repair (average): $150-400
